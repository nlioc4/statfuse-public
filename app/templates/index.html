Welcome to StatFuse


Characters

The Characters page is mainly used to store your characters in browser cookies. This lets you use the same characters on all of the report pages without needing to type them on each page. This page also gives easy access to Fisu and Honu links for each character.

General Infantry Weapons(beta)

The report pages generate tables and charts to showcase different data.
General - common all-time stats such as time played, kills, k/d, etc.
Infantry - stats based on infantry classes such as how many times you have killed a medic or died to an infil, etc.
Weapons(beta) - hastily added page to show medal distribution since I like to see auraxium medals.

What is StatFuse?

StatFuse is a site made to easily provide statistics from all* of your characters, including totals/averages. This is primarily designed to report on alt characters, but the tables and charts do also provide the ability to easily compare multiple characters.

*may or may not be able to handle all your characters, some of you nutcases have more than the 16 that I allow you to run a report with.

One thing to note, I have never made a website before and this is my first large project. I expect some issues will pop up and QoL features will be missing. Please reach out to me with the information below if you have any errors/requests.
